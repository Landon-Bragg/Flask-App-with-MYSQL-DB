About My Web App

This Flask web app allows customers of the movie rental service to access the full records of their own rental history. Additionally, users are able to analyze movies that they are interested in renting. They can get a better understanding of movies they might choose to rent. This web app can also be useful for managerial staff who want to see their most loyal customers and the seasonality and trends of rental data at their stores. It was developed by Landon Bragg, Zak Khan, Ben Kim, and Conner Stallman.
